Want to be a route manager?

Route managers help coordinate members in their area to ensure goods are delivered to their community on delivery day. They are responsible for recruiting volunteer drivers each month and ensuring the members on their route know where and when to collect their goods on Delivery Day.

We'd love to have your support. Please notify our admin with the contact page and we can help you arrange your route.
