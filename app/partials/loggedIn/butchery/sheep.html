How it works

Once you click the 'Place Order' button at the bottom of the page, your instructions and order will be sent directly to the producer. If they need any other details from you the producer will contact you by email. You will receive an email as well to be a record of your order.

Then the producer will begin work on your order. Once the producer knows the final weight and price for your order, they'll update your order on our website and you'll receive an invoice with delivery/pickup instructions.
