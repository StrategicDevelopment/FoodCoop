Thank you for deciding to order your {{beast.productName}} through the Northland Natural Food Co-op. Whenever you order a whole or half {{beast.productName}} you'll need to fill out this form. It contains the instructions the butchers need to know how to prepare your meat.

How it works

Once you click the 'Place Order' button at the bottom of the page, your instructions and order will be sent directly to the producer. If they need any other details from you the producer will contact you by email. You will receive an email as well to be a record of your order.

Then the producer will begin work on your order. Once the producer has completed your order they will notify you and you can pick it up on the next delivery day. Because slaughter schedules may not always line up with our delivery day schedule, you may not be able to get your meat order at the same time as your other shopping items.
