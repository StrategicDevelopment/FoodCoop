What is a food co-op?

By definition, a co-op is owned by its members. Members of the Northland Natural Food Co-op, will be participating in the creation of a local not-for-profit food system in Northland. The Northland NFC is based on existing working models around the world. See the Oklahoma Food Co-op and Ottawa Valley Food Co-op for examples.

How does our Co-op Work?

  1. Each month, our producer members post fresh products on our website
  2. Members then orders from the various producers
  3. On Delivery Day orders get collected from Whangarei or a central location in their community
