Want an easy way to

buy & sell local food?


As a member of the Northland Natural Food Coop, you can buy and sell local food through our online stores. Simply upload your products or place your order online. The food is then delivered to our sorting centers, and distributed to pick-up sites all over Northland for customers to collect.

Want assurance your food is

fresh, healthy and local?


As a customer, you will know exactly who produced your food, as well as where and how it was grown or raised. This gives you a unique opportunity to support the producers and production practices of your choice.

Want a fair price for your products?


As a producer, you will have complete control over the sale of your products. You set the prices; you decide what to sell, when to sell, and how much to sell. Regardless of your scale of operation, you will be connected with consumers who value quality products, healthy local economies, and a fair income for your work.


100% member owned


The NNFC is 100% member owned and operated, so by signing up, you also become a shareholder. You will receive a percentage of the co-op’s profits each year, and have the right to vote on all major decisions. For details, see our policy handbook

Become an Owner


After a 30-day trial, the one time cost of joining our co-op makes you an official shareholder of the Food Co-op. Benefits of being a shareholder include lifetime membership in the NNFC, unlimimited access to our exclusive online store, the right to vote on all major decisions about the co-op, and receive a percentage of the co-op’s profits each year.

Membership is 100% refundable and only mandatory after a 30-day trial. This trial begins the first time you buy or sell something online with us.
