Want an easy way to buy & sell local food?

As a member of the Northland Natural Food Coop, you can buy and sell local food through our online store. Simply upload your products or place your order online. The food is then delivered to our offices, and distributed to pick-up sites all over Northland for customers to collect.

Want assurance your food is fresh, healthy and local?

As a customer, you will know exactly who produced your food, as well as where and how it was grown or raised. This gives you a unique opportunity to support the producers and production practices of your choice.

Want a fair price for your products?

As a producer, you will have complete control over the sale of your products. You set the prices; you decide what to sell, when to sell, and how much to sell. Regardless of your scale of operation, you will be connected with consumers who value quality products, healthy local economies, and a fair income for your work.

We are a 100% member owned social enterprise

The NNFC is 100% member owned and operated, so by signing up, you also become a shareholder. You will receive a percentage of the co-op’s profits each year, and have the right to vote on all major decisions.

For details, see our policy handbook

Pay just once. No yearly fee or subscription

The one time cost of joining gives you and your household lifetime membership in the NNFC. You will gain access to our exclusive online store, have the right to vote on all major decisions about the co-op, and receive a percentage of the co-op’s profits each year.
