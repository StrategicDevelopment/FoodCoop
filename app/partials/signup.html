Members all own one share of the Northland Natural Food Co-op

This share entitles the holder to vote at the AGM and any other shareholder meetings of the NNFC as outlined in the constitution. It also entitles the shareholder’s household to participate in services run and organised by the NNFC for member’s benifit.

As we are a grassroots movement attempting to keep costs down, we do not have any systems for handling payments through our website at this time. Should you wish to be a member, we will send you an invoice via email. We would appreciate you paying for this as soon as possible as the money goes to support our services and costs of business.

Please note that membership is 100% refundable should you no longer wish to be a member.
